Delhi to Kasol: A Himalayan Adventure by Bus

By Wanderlust Wheels Staff · JULY 23, 2025

Why Take the Bus from Delhi to Kasol?

Traveling by bus from Delhi to kasol is more than just a journey—it’s an experience! Watch the city fade into rolling hills, pass through pine forests, and catch glimpses of the Beas River winding through the valley. Whether you’re a solo traveler, a group of friends, or a family, the bus ride is a chance to relax, make new friends, and soak in the changing landscapes.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here’s everything you need to know for a smooth and memorable trip.

Journey & Timings

Q: How long does it take to travel from Delhi to Manali by bus?
A: The journey covers about 510–551 km and usually takes 10–12 hours, depending on traffic and weather.

Q: What are the main departure times from Delhi?
A: Buses depart throughout the day, with popular options in the evening and late night. Check our website for the latest schedule.

Fares & Booking

Q: How much does a Delhi to Manali bus ticket cost?
A: Fares start from around ₹690, depending on the bus type. Early booking often gets you the best price.

Q: Are there any discounts or offers?
A: Yes! We regularly feature special deals and app-exclusive discounts.

Your Manali Checklist: Sights, Food & More

Must-Visit Places in Manali

  • Hadimba Temple: A peaceful, ancient temple surrounded by cedar trees.
  • Solang Valley: The adventure hub for skiing, paragliding, and zorbing.
  • Old Manali: Known for its chill cafes, live music, and bohemian vibe.
