About PNR Guru

Your trusted partner for real-time train information.

Welcome to PNR Guru, India's premier platform for live PNR status and train tracking. Our mission is to simplify your train travel experience by providing accurate, real-time information at your fingertips. We understand the importance of timely updates when it comes to train journeys, and we are committed to delivering the most reliable data to ensure peace of mind for every passenger.

Founded with the vision of making railway information accessible and user-friendly, PNR Guru leverages cutting-edge technology to connect directly with Indian Railways' live database. This allows us to offer instant PNR status checks, live train running status, detailed passenger information, and much more, all within a secure and intuitive interface.

Our Features Include:

  • Lightning Fast PNR Status: Get instant updates on your booking confirmation, coach, and berth details.
  • Real-time Live Tracking: Track your train's current location, previous and next stations, and real-time delay information.
  • Comprehensive Passenger Details: View detailed information for all passengers under a single PNR.
  • Secure and Reliable: Your data privacy and security are our top priorities, with encrypted connections and no personal data storage.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed for ease of use across all devices, ensuring a seamless experience.

At PNR Guru, we are continuously working to enhance our services and introduce new features that add value to your journey. We are dedicated to being your go-to source for all Indian Railways information. Thank you for choosing PNR Guru!
